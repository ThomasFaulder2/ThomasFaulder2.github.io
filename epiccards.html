The EPIC Cards

Emergency Physician's Important Cases

A resource by Zafar Smith

The EPIC cards are flashcards with emergency medicine case studies. The cards were created by Dr Zafar Smith, FACEM, as an educational resource for medical students.

This app enables you to:

  • Study the EPIC cards as flashcards.
  • Search all cards.
  • View EPIC cards in a PDF format.
  • Publish your own EPIC cards and leave comments on other students' submissions.

The app allows students to publish case studies. These case studies are stored in an online database. These cases are fictional and cannot be linked back to patients.
