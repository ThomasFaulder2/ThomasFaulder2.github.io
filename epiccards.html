Epic Cards

A resource by Zafar Smith

The EPIC cards, or "Emergency Physician's Important Cases," are flashcards with emergency medicine case studies. The cards were created by Dr Zafar Smith, FACEM. The cards were created as a learning tool for medical students.

This app enables you to:

  • Study the EPIC cards as flashcards.
  • Search all cards.
  • View EPIC cards in a PDF format.
  • Publish your own EPIC cards and leave comments on other students' submissions.
